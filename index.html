Welcome to Hamster Stackers!

Help HamHam to save his friends falling from the tree.

Use the arrow right & left keys to control HamHam's movement to catch his friends.

Click to ask the next hamster to jump. Try to click now, the first hamster is a trial run.

There should be 5 hamsters stacked to win the game. If one falls, you lose the game.

Refresh after every win/lose session to avoid any glitches.
